🇹🇷 VISION – Smart Productivity Lab

We plan to create Turkey's first smart coffee lab merging specialty coffee and infrastructure for remote professionals. NEURA Coffee Lab – a space where focus meets exceptional coffee.

Hybrid work market
800k remote workers in Istanbul

📉 MARKET GAP

Bomonti hosts thousands of freelancers and entrepreneurs, but traditional cafes are noisy and lack infrastructure (outlets, fast internet). Co‑working spaces cost 10x more.

🧬 PROPOSED SOLUTION

  • Custom benches with Type-C/wireless charging.
  • Ultra-fast internet (fiber + 5G backup).
  • Noise-cancelling sound zones.
  • B2B digital subscription platform.

📍 GEOGRAPHIC EDGE

  • Bomonti – “Brooklyn of Istanbul”, high density of startups and students.
  • Less than 5 minutes from Mimar Sinan University and metro stations.
  • No direct competitor offering a “productivity lab”.
